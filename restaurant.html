Several of the areas in The One Palácio da Anunciada maintain the original essence of the building and are an ideal setting for you to enjoy the gastronomy of the Portuguese capital. The groundbreaking Condes de Ericeira restaurant, located in one of the palace's noble rooms, offers a refined menu featuring traditional Portuguese market cuisine with hints of the Mediterranean. At O Jardim Wine Bar, which provides direct access to the Palace Gardens, you can enjoy a signature cocktail or an exquisite breakfast based on natural products. You'll also find the Boémio Lounge in the hotel lobby: a spot with large windows and the original wrought iron columns, the perfect space for any type of private event.
